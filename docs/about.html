⚜ Scout Oath

On my honor I will do my best
To do my duty to God and my country
and to obey the Scout Law;
To help other people at all times;
To keep myself physically strong,
mentally awake, and morally straight.

⚜ Scout Law

A Scout is:

✦ Trustworthy✦ Loyal ✦ Helpful✦ Friendly ✦ Courteous✦ Kind ✦ Obedient✦ Cheerful ✦ Thrifty✦ Brave ✦ Clean✦ Reverent

What We Do

🏕️

Outdoor Adventures

Monthly campouts, hikes, canoe trips, and high-adventure experiences that build teamwork and self-reliance.

🎖️

Merit Badge Program

Scouts explore over 130 merit badges in areas including STEM, arts, athletics, and citizenship.

🤝

Community Service

Regular service projects that give back to the community while teaching the value of service to others.

🦅

Eagle Scout Path

Our structured program guides motivated Scouts through the ranks to Scouting's highest honor.

👥

Leadership Development

Scouts take on troop leadership roles that develop confidence, responsibility, and communication skills.

🎉

Courts of Honor

Regular ceremonies celebrating Scout achievements, rank advancements, and merit badge completions.

Interested in Joining?

We welcome Scouts ages 11–17 (or who have completed 5th grade). The best way to get started is to attend one of our Tuesday evening meetings as a guest.

Parents are encouraged to get involved — we're always looking for adult volunteers to help lead, mentor, and support the troop.
